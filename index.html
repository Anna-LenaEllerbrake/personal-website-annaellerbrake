About Anna

Hi, I am Anna! I first got in touch with coding in school. Back then one of my graduation subjects was computer science and I loved learning some java and mysql, so it was no surprise that when I started studying mathematics, I chose computer science as sidesubject. During this time I started a job as a working student in my current company SchulLV, where I wrote digital text books in mathematics and exams for school. After my studies I became content manager and shareholder of this company and somehow lost track of my second passion coding and programming.

Now as we needed support in our coding team at SchulLV it was clear that i am the right person to switch the job, go through a web development bootcamp and learn the basics! That was the right decision in my responsibility as a shareholder and it was the one ultimative chance for me personal to tollow my passion for programming. I am very excited to learn a bunch of new things and looking forward to the future where I will be responsible for SchulLVs website and mobile apps!
